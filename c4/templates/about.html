About this app

A simple analytics tool that turns uploaded files into clean summaries and charts.

What it does
- Detects columns and data types
- Shows missing values and numeric summaries
- Highlights top values for categorical columns
Charts
Build charts from your uploaded CSV: pie, bar, histogram, scatter, and line charts.
Tip
For the best charts, choose a categorical column for X (pie/bar) and a numeric column for Y (bar/scatter/line).
